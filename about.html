ISO 9001 : 2015 is the international standard that specifies requirements for Quality Management System (QMS). Opus Technology Limited is an ISO 9001 : 2015, following their high standards for consistently providing products and services to customers to which other organizations can certify.

SO 27001:2022 Certified

Being Certified indicates that an organization has implemented an information security management system (ISMS) in accordance with the requirements specified in the 2022 version of the ISO 27001 standard. This certification signifies that the organization has established and maintains effective controls and processes to manage information security risks, protect sensitive data, and ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information assets. Achieving ISO 27001 certification demonstrates a commitment to information security and can enhance trust and confidence among stakeholders, customers, and partners. However, as of my last update, I don’t have specific details on the changes or updates introduced in the 2022 version of the ISO 27001 standard.
